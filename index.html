STRANGE MEET

A meeting of strange results.

In the last few years the number of awesome and amazing, can't be missed conferences has seemed to sky rocket. Strange Meet is a gathering where we are inviting a bunch of our friends to present about whatever topic they want, without the restrictions and pressures of a themed event. We're also hoping that Strange Meet will be an opportunity for all our friends to get a chance to share some of their knowledge in front of an audience.

The day will consist of a series of talks, workshops, exhibitions and lots of meeting some strange new people.

Why should I come?

It might be a total waste of time but we'll be providing three free meals either way.

When and where is this waste of time happening?

All day starting at 10 AM on June 11th in the Dumbo area of Brooklyn, NY. Exact location and schedule details will follow in an email closer to the date.

Who is will be there?

Our goal for this mini conference is to put a lot of our friends who don't know each other in the same space.

If you are reading this page you are probably already friends with (or a friend-of-a-friend of) one of the organizers.

What will be discussed?

We don't know yet. We're inviting a lot of cool and interesting people and asking them to talk about whatever they want.

Some possible topics may include:

  • [Weird] Science
  • [Codes of] Law
  • [Blood] Sports
  • [Unsolved] History
  • [Vernacular] Architecture
  • [Analog] Textiles
  • [Dark] Networking
  • [Confusing] Art
